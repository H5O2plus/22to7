problem solving

The aim of this program is to teach problem solving techniques in a fun and enganging way that will change how you will fundamentally think about math. I hope that after each lesson, you will gain a new mental tool that you can apply to many math problems that would arise through high school and college. Competition math will be covered, but our primary focus will be to learn general mathematical problem solving skills that traditional curricular math classes does not cover.
